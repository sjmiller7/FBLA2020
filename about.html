About Us

At Mercury, we are devoted to giving our travelers outstanding flight services at an extremely affordable cost. Mercury Airlines has been apart of almost all south-eastern airports since 1992. Our airline started as a way to make airlines more affordable and more accessible to everyone in need of airfare. However, as our company has developed our airline has become much more than that. Our program has devoted itself to not only make airlines more affordable, but to also ensure that our customers receive the best flight experience possible.
